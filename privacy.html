Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how MyLLama handles information when you use the app.

Summary

  • MyLLama is a chat client that connects to Ollama servers you configure.
  • Your messages are sent to the Ollama server address you provide, in order to generate responses.
  • Chat history may be stored on your device so you can review conversations by chat room.

Information We Collect

MyLLama does not require account registration. The app may store the following locally on your device:

  • Ollama server addresses you add (e.g., URL/host and port)
  • Chat room titles and chat messages (conversation history)
  • Basic app settings

How Your Data Is Used

  • To connect to your Ollama server and send your prompts/messages.
  • To display responses returned by your server.
  • To keep conversation history organized by chat room on your device.

Data Sharing

MyLLama does not sell your data. Messages are transmitted to the Ollama server you configure. If your server is hosted by a third party or on a remote machine, that server operator may have access to the data you send.

Data Storage & Retention

Conversation history and server addresses are stored locally on your device unless you remove them. You can delete chat rooms to remove stored messages within the app.

Security

Security depends on how your Ollama server is deployed and protected. If you connect to a remote server, consider using trusted networks, firewalls, and secure transport where applicable.

Children’s Privacy

MyLLama is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top indicates the latest revision.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact the developer via the support channel listed on the App Store product page.
